# Beach Houses In Mayaro 

## Purpose

This is the repository for the an online booking agency that specifically deals with bookings in Mayaro Trinidad. The webpage consist of the following on a SPA:

- A Homepage
- An About
- A Contact Page

There also exist a separate blog page.

## Instructions

A user can make a booking by clicking the "Book Now" CTA button on the homepage or by scroll down directly to the booking page. In addition a user can also click on an internal link located in the blog article. After entering the revalant credentials on the booking page (name, email, check-in, check-out and guest) they must the click the check for available booking CTA which submits the data to backend and customer is then subsequent emailed with a payment link and images of the room to be booked.
